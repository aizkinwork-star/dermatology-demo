FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions before your visit. Call the clinic if yours isn't answered here.

How much does consultation cost?

Consultation fees vary by treatment type. Call the clinic or message on WhatsApp for current pricing.

Is laser treatment painful?

Most patients describe a mild warming or tingling sensation. Numbing cream is available for sensitive areas.

What are the clinic timings?

The clinic is open Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Closed on Sundays.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Appointments are recommended to avoid wait times. Walk-ins are accommodated based on availability.

How many sessions will I need for hair loss treatment?

This depends on the cause and severity of hair loss. The doctor will outline a session plan after your first consultation.

What payment methods are accepted?

Cash, UPI, and card payments are accepted at the clinic.
